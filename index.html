Methodology

Over the course of a year, from 1/20/15 to 1/20/16, every instance of defecation was recorded in a spreadsheet. Defecation, I defined as, a sit, a loaf, a wipe, and a flush. Courtusy flushes, half an hour slugfests, and multiple wipe and poo sessions did not increase the tally more than once. I recorded four statistics regarding each poop, date, time, location, and consistancy. Consistancy was measured via eyeball, in reference to the Bristol School Scale . Raw data can be found here.
